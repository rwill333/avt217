XD Instructions

  1. Open XD.
  2. Create a 500px by 500px artboard.
  3. Draw a 350px by 350px square aligned with the center of the artboard.
  4. Remove the fill but leave the size 1 border.
  5. Draw a 200px by 200px square aligned with the center of the artboard.
  6. Fill with #4E2CBF and remove the border.
  7. Draw two 100px by 100px circles.
  8. Fill both circles with #C91E1E and remove the border.
  9. Place the first circle at 100 X and 100 Y.
  10. Place the second circle at 300 X and 300 Y.
  11. Export as PNG.
